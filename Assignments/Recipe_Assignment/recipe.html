Delicious Chocolate Cake

This chocolate cake is rich, moist, and perfect for all chocolate lovers. It's easy to make and guaranteed to impress your friends and family.

Total Cook Time: 1 hour 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 cup of cocoa powder
  • 1.5 cups of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 0.5 cup of vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).

    Time: 10 minutes

  2. Mix the flour, cocoa powder, sugar, baking powder, and salt.

    Time: 10 minutes

  3. Add the milk, eggs, oil, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients.

    Time: 10 minutes

  4. Pour the batter into a greased cake pan.

    Time: 5 minutes

  5. Bake for 35 minutes in the preheated oven.

    Time: 35 minutes

  6. Let the cake cool before serving.

    Time: 20 minutes
